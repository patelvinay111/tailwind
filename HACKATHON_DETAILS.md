## What You're Building

Build a voice agent that shows up when it matters most — not a chatbot, not a notification, but a real voice agent.

A real trip isn't built with one booking. There's the flight, the hotel, rideshares, dinner reservations, concert tickets, and the five confirmation emails you'll never find again. Every leg lives in a different app. None of them talk to each other.

**Your job: make them talk.** Prove that flights, stays, ground transport, dining, and other experiences can be pulled into a single conversation, a single itinerary, all booked and managed by voice AI.

### Requirements

- Must use **Sabre APIs** and **Vocal Bridge** to qualify for prizes
- Live demos are the Vocal Bridge way, however they come out

### Example Ideas

- Agent that speaks fluent Japanese and English to unscramble a hotel snafu in Osaka after landing at 2am
- Agent that literally calls the airline on your behalf, waits on hold, and speaks to a human agent to rebook your cancelled flight
